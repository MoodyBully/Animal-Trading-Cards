Staffordshire Bull Terrier

Although they were created in 19th century Britain to be a small, fast figting dog, those days are long past. The Staffordshire Bull Terrer of today is a fine companion known for thier courage, intelligence, and love of children.

  • Country of Origin: England
  • Weight: 11-17kg
  • Average Lifespan: 12-14 years
  • Temperament: Intelligent, Bold, Loyal

A stocky, muscular, and unusally strong small to medium sized dog. Nicknamed, Staffy, they have a broad chest, strong shoulders, well-boned wide-set legs, a medium length tail that is carried low and broad head with a short muzzle. Thier ears fold over at the tips and are not cropped. The Staffordshire Bull Terriers' coat is short, stiff, and close. Base colors can be red, fawn, white, black, or blue, or any one of the aforementioned colors with white.
